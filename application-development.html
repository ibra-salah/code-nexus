Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile app development service

How long does it take to build a mobile app?
A simple MVP can be ready in 4–6 weeks. A full-featured professional app typically takes 8–14 weeks, and complex enterprise apps may take 3–6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
Should I build a native or cross-platform app?
Cross-platform (Flutter or React Native) is ideal for most businesses — it saves cost and time while delivering near-native performance. Native development is recommended for apps requiring advanced hardware access or peak performance, like games or camera-heavy apps.
Do you handle App Store and Play Store submission?
Yes. We manage the entire submission process for both stores, including preparing screenshots, descriptions, ASO metadata, and handling any review feedback from Apple or Google.
Will my app work offline?
Yes, if required. We can implement local data caching and offline mode so your users can continue using core features without an internet connection, syncing data when connectivity is restored.
What happens after the app is launched?
We provide post-launch support covering bug fixes, OS update compatibility, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. All packages include a support period, and we offer extended maintenance contracts as well.
