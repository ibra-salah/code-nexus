Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our web development service

How long does it take to build a website?
Timelines vary by project scope. A simple landing page takes 3–7 days, a business website 2–4 weeks, and a full e-commerce or custom platform 4–10 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before starting.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all screen sizes — mobile, tablet, and desktop — using a mobile-first development approach.
Do you provide hosting and domain services?
We can guide you through selecting and setting up hosting and domain registration. We work with most major providers and can assist with DNS configuration and deployment.
Can I update the website content myself?
Yes. We can integrate a CMS (Content Management System) so you can easily update text, images, and content without touching any code. We'll also provide a walkthrough on how to use it.
What information do you need to start?
We start with a discovery call to understand your goals, target audience, preferred design style, and functionality requirements. From there we create a proposal and timeline before any work begins.
